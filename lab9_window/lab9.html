elephant

The elephant is the largest land animal on Earth.It has a long trunk used for breathing, smelling, drinking, and grabbing food.Elephants are very intelligent and have strong memories.They live in groups called herds, usually led by a female.There are two main species — African and Asian elephants.Elephants love to play in water and often spray themselves to stay cool.Sadly, they are endangered due to poaching and loss of habitat.

Tigars

The tiger is a powerful and majestic wild animal known for its striking orange coat with black stripes. It is the largest member of the cat family and is admired for its strength and grace. Tigers are skilled hunters that usually live alone and hunt at night, feeding on deer, wild boar, and other animals. They are found mainly in forests and grasslands across Asia. Despite their beauty and importance in nature, tigers are an endangered species due to hunting and habitat loss. Protecting tigers is important to maintain the balance of our ecosystem and preserve wildlife for future generations.
