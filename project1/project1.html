🐶 DOG

A dog barks loudly with a cheerful “woof-woof.” It loves to eat meat, bones, and sometimes biscuits. Dogs come in many sizes, from tiny to very big. They have soft fur and a wagging tail that shows joy.

×

🐱 CAT

A cat meows softly with “meow-meow.” It likes milk, fish, and small animals. Cats are graceful and small with shiny eyes. They are curious, clean, and love to nap.

×

🐮 COW

A cow makes a deep “moo-moo” sound. It eats grass and hay all day. Cows are large, gentle animals with kind eyes. They are calm and move slowly in groups.

×

🐑 SHEEP

A sheep bleats with “baa-baa.” It eats grass and plants in the fields. Sheep are fluffy with soft white wool. They are peaceful and like to stay in herds.

×

🐰 RABBIT

A rabbit makes soft “sniff-sniff” sounds. It eats carrots, leaves, and vegetables. Rabbits are small with long ears and soft fur. They are shy and hop quickly when scared.

×

🐨 KOALA

A koala makes a deep “grunting” sound. It eats eucalyptus leaves high in trees. Koalas are gray, small, and fluffy. They are gentle and sleep most of the day.

×

🦁 LION

A lion roars loudly across the jungle. It eats meat and lives in a pride. Lions are large with golden fur and a mane. They are brave and strong leaders of the wild.

×

🐘 ELEPHANT

An elephant trumpets with its trunk. It eats leaves, fruits, and grass. Elephants are huge with gray skin and tusks. They are smart, gentle, and love their family.

×

🦊 FOX

A fox makes a “yip-yip” sound. It eats berries and small animals. Foxes are small, fast, and clever. They explore quietly at night.

×

🐼 PANDA

A panda makes a soft “growl” sound. It eats bamboo and rests a lot. Pandas are black and white and very cute. They are calm and love to climb trees.
